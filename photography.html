ABOUT MY PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography is my way of capturing the world as I see it - through moments of beauty, emotion, and storytelling. Each image represents a unique perspective, a frozen moment in time that speaks to the viewer.

My work spans across various genres including street photography, landscapes, portraits, and urban exploration. I believe in the power of visual storytelling and strive to create images that evoke emotion and inspire others.

Based in Mumbai, India, I find inspiration in the bustling city life, the changing seasons, and the incredible diversity of people and places around me.
