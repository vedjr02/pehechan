About Me.

Hi, I'm Vedant, a passionate B.Tech engineering graduate driven by curiosity and creativity. I specialize in building intelligent systems, with hands-on experience in computer vision and AI projects like gesture-controlled interfaces and virtual drawing tools. Beyond engineering, I have a keen eye for visual storytelling through photography, blending technology and art in my personal and academic work. I'm currently preparing for an MSc in Data Analytics at the National College of Ireland, aiming to dive deeper into data-driven innovation.
